Freecell help

Preferences

There are two options available in the freecell preferences which can make playing easier.

Automatically move cards to stacks
With this option on, cards which are no longer needed for the game will be moved to the stacks when they become available. Aces are always stacked, and other cards are stacked if they cannot be needed later in the game.
Allow multiple-card moves
This option allows you to make multiple-card moves from column to column with only two clicks. You can only move as many cards as you could manually, but it saves a lot of busy-work.
